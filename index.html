Market
Demands
Let’s admit, when the classical methods of analysis do not work, and there are no regulators, the Wolf from Wall Street is uncomfortable to feel like a hamster on a roller coaster of stock quotes.

That’s exactly why

  • Traders are searching for tools, that would easily visualize the results of all their trading operations on exchanges
  • Portfolio managers are searching for intelligible ways to diversify trusted capital with clear visualization of the results
  • Investors are searching for a trustworthy analysis of future events, and not a statement of what happened
  • The market requires a scheme of values and an ICO flow rating
Solution
In addition, we intend to form a qualified community whose goal will be exclusively to trade, invest and get profit with the use of crypto-currencies and projects, using crypto-currencies.
We find our platform’s product monetization in the application of the business model by subscription – SaaS (Software as a Service) with an integrated exchange of opportunities for crypto units. Being a community not pursuing the benefits of the fiat world, we declare that all mutual settlements of the platform will be available only in crypto units without linking to the rate of any fiat pair. The community considers itself not having a tax residency and legal form, and its declared founders are only the first among equal participants.

The platform to be created will provide:

  • Traders with a tool for monitoring the results of trading on available exchanges, tracking the changes in marginality from the start of trading or select a period
  • Portfolio managers with a tool for convenient management of their clients’ portfolios, applying known trading strategies and not worrying about their incorrect display and generating detailed reporting for clients;
  • Investors with analytics to comprehend the situation on the market and an availability to use the platform-based analytics for trading and investments – short-term, medium-term and long-term
  • Bots with the necessary information and API for profitable high-frequency trading
  • Automatically generated recommendations, estimating risks of investing in the announced ICOs
